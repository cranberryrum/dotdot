# DOTDOT — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 21, 2026_

DOTDOT ("the app", "we", "us") is a small app for sending tiny dot‑grid drawings and
photos to your friends' Home Screen widgets. This policy explains what the app
stores, why, and the choices you have. We've kept it plain and honest.

## The short version

- **No accounts, no logins, no passwords.** Your identity is your iCloud account.
- **We don't track you, run ads, or sell your data.** There are no analytics or
  advertising SDKs in the app.
- Your data is stored in **Apple's iCloud (CloudKit)** and is used only to deliver
  your messages to the friends you choose.

## What the app stores

When you use DOTDOT, the following is stored in the app's Apple CloudKit container:

- **Your profile** — the name and identity token (an emoji or letter, and a color)
  you pick. This is shown to friends you pair with.
- **An identifier** — your iCloud user‑record identifier and a random per‑device
  value, used only to deliver messages to the right device. It is not used for
  advertising or cross‑app tracking.
- **Friend connections** — a record linking your identity to a friend's, created
  when you pair using a one‑time 6‑digit code.
- **Drawings and photos you send** — the 8×8 dot patterns, and any photo you frame
  and send. Photos are downscaled to a small thumbnail size before sending. These
  are stored so they can be delivered to the recipient.

DOTDOT does not read your photo library on its own — you choose a specific photo
through Apple's system picker, which does not give the app access to your whole
library. The camera is used only if you choose to take a photo.

## Why we store it

Solely to provide the app's core function: letting you pair with friends and send
drawings and photos to their widgets. We have no other use for this data.

## Who can see it

- Friends you pair with can see your name, your token, and the drawings or photos
  you send **to them**.
- Your data is processed by **Apple's iCloud / CloudKit** on your behalf. We do not
  share it with any other third party.

## Retention and deletion

- A message you send is stored until it is delivered and/or until you delete your
  data.
- You can delete your data at any time in **Settings → Delete my data**. This removes
  your profile, your friend connections, and the drawings you've sent from CloudKit,
  and resets the app on your device.
- **Please note:** a drawing or photo that has already been delivered to a friend's
  device may still exist on that device, which is outside our control.

## Children

DOTDOT is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal
information from them.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above will
change. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
